`grid-template-rows` Variations

1. `grid-template-rows: none;`

No explicit grid rows are defined. Rows are implicitly generated, and their size is determined by `grid-auto-rows` (which defaults to `auto` if not set).

2. `grid-template-rows: 100px 1fr;` (Fixed and Flexible)

Defines two rows: the first is 100px tall, and the second takes up the remaining available space (1 fraction).

3. `grid-template-rows: 50px 100px 75px;` (Multiple Fixed Sizes)

Defines three rows with specific pixel heights.

Row 1 (50px)

4. `grid-template-rows: [header-start] 50px [header-end] [content-start] 1fr [content-end];`

Defines rows with explicit line names. This allows for easier placement of grid items using these names.

5. `grid-template-rows: 1fr 2fr 1fr;`

Defines three flexible rows. The second row will be twice as tall as the first and third rows, sharing available space proportionally.

Row 1 (1fr)
Row 2 (2fr)
Row 3 (1fr)

6. `grid-template-rows: minmax(50px, 100px);`

Defines a single row that will be at least 50px tall and at most 100px tall. If content requires more than 100px, it will overflow, but the track itself won't exceed 100px.

Short Content
Longer Content
This will be clipped or overflow if container is smaller than content needs.

7. `grid-template-rows: minmax(min-content, max-content);`

Defines a row that will be sized between its minimum and maximum content contributions.

Min Content
Max Content Example with more text.

8. `grid-template-rows: max-content min-content;`

The first row takes the largest maximal content contribution of its items. The second row takes the largest minimal content contribution of its items.

9. `grid-template-rows: auto;`

The row will take up any remaining space and behaves similarly to `minmax(min-content, max-content)`. It can also be stretched by `align-content`.

Item 2

10. `grid-template-rows: fit-content(150px);`

The row size is calculated as `min(max-content, max(auto, 150px))`. It means the row will be at most 150px tall, but will at least accommodate its auto content.

Short Content
This content is longer and might exceed 150px, but the track itself won't grow beyond 150px due to `fit-content`.

`grid-template-rows: unset;`

If the property is inherited, it behaves like `inherit`. Otherwise, it behaves like `initial`.
